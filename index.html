The gas estimates shown above are calculated based on the total number of stablecoin transactions processed through StabiX and compared against the lowest average transaction fees across Ethereum, EVM chains(Consider Here Only Arbitrum,Base and Polygon), and Tron under normal network conditions. If these same transactions were executed directly on chain, users would have incurred the equivalent network gas costs shown above. During network congestion, transaction fees can increase significantly beyond the normal average. On Ethereum, gas costs may rise between 2x to 10x, on EVM chains between 2x to 5x, and on Tron between 2x to 4x depending on network demand and activity. StabiX eliminates these recurring stablecoin transfer transaction (gas)costs by maintaining an internal off chain transaction environment backed by on chain reserves, allowing users to send and receive stablecoins instantly with zero gas fees inside the ecosystem.
